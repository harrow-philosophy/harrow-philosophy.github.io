## Definitions:
 - Origin of Property: A man living and satisfying his wants by the ceaseless application of [man's] faculties to natural resources.

- Origin of Plunder: a man living and satisfying his wants by seizing and consuming the products of the labor of others.

Socialism = Legal Plunder
- Legal Plunder: The plunder of property by means of legislation, ie. State plunder.
